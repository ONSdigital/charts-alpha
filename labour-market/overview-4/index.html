Key statistics:

Employment

  • There are 32.6 million people in work.
  • The employment rate is 75.8%.
  • It increased by 0.6% since last quarter.
  • It's the joint highest since estimates began in 1971.

Unemployment

  • There are 1.36 million people unemployed.
  • The unemployment rate is 4%.
  • It has decreased by 0.3% in the last quarter
  • It has not been lower since 1975.
