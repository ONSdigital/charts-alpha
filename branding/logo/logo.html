The Office for National Statistics logo contains three elements, symbol, wording and colours.

To ensure consistent legibility and clarity of the identity, the logo comes in three versions. In each instance, the identity has been refined to enhance its usage with certain media or at different sizes.

  1. Primary logo
  2. Landscape logo
  3. Symbol

Primary logo

Use this whenever possible.

Landscape logo

Online and social media use only. Use this version of the logo when the vertical space is limited.

Symbol

Use when horizontal space is limited e.g. social media avatar or fav icon
