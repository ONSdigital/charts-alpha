Secondary font family

Arial

In circumstances where Frutiger typefaces are unavailable the Arial typeface family only must be used.

The Arial typeface is used widely throughout ONS and is the standard font for corporate PowerPoint and Microsoft Word templates.

Generally speaking headings and subheadings may be set in Bold style and body copy should be set in Regular style.
