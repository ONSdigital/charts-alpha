Online font family

Open Sans

The ONS online font is Open Sans. This typeface should be used when producing content for all ONS digital channels.

Open Sans is a sans serif typeface, neutral and friendly in appearance. It has been optimised for web and mobile interfaces, and has excellent legibility in its letterforms

To ensure consistency and accessibility, the Open Sans family has been limited to 3 weights: Open Sans Regular, Open Sans Semi-bold and Open Sans Bold.

Print font family

Dax family

Frutiger family

This typeface is to be used when producing ONS corporate branded print materials.

To ensure consistency, the Frutiger family has been limited to 3 weights: Frutiger 45 Light, Frutiger Roman and Frutiger 65 Bold.

If the Frutiger font is not available the secondary corporate font Arial should be used. This is common for documents produced internally.
