Online font family

Open Sans

The ONS online font is Open Sans. This typeface should be used when producing content for all ONS digital channels.

Open Sans is a sans serif typeface, neutral and friendly in appearance. It has been optimised for web and mobile interfaces, and has excellent legibility in its letterforms

To ensure consistency and accessibility, the Open Sans family has been limited to 3 weights: Open Sans Regular, Open Sans Semi-bold and Open Sans Bold.

Print font family

Dax family

Dax is our logo font to be used by the ONS Design team or external design agencies. It is used to enhance ONS branded print materials only.

Dax should be used for header text or primary content which need to stand out on a page. Don't use Dax for body copy.

To ensure consistency and accessibility, the Dax family has been limited to 3 weights: ODax Regular, Dax Medium and Dax Bold.

Frutiger family

This typeface should be used when producing ONS corporate branded print materials.

To ensure consistency, the Frutiger family has been limited to 3 weights: Frutiger 45 Light, Frutiger Roman and Frutiger 65 Bold.

Generally speaking, Frutiger Bold should be used for sub headers, Frutiger Light for body copy 13pt or above and Frutiger Roman for 12pt body copy of below.

If Frutiger is not available the secondary corporate font Arial should be used. This is common for documents produced internally.

Accessible communications

For the purpose of providing accessible print outputs a minimum font size of 12pt should be used wherever possible.
