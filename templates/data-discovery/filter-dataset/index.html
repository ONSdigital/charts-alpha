Iterations

Heading

Usability testing has helped to improve the heading 1 on this page. Originally it was "Topics in this dataset" but this didn't test well. "Customise this dataset" was better un derstood, but sounded like "hard work" to users. "Filter this dataset" is better and seems to fit the user's mential model.

Filter links

Similarly, the links on the page were changed from "Change" to "Customise". Finally they were changed to "Filter" to match the user's mental model of the process.

Primary button

Changed from "Continue to download" to "Choose a download format" to better describe the process.
