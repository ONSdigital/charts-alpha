Landscape logo

To ensure clarity, legibility of the identity and to maximise the content area of a social media graphic, the Office for National Statistics landscape logo should be used at all times.

The single colour black version should be used on white and light backgrounds. This ensures the identity is clear, yet secondary to the content and messages contained within the graphic.

The reverse white logo should be used on dark coloured backgrounds. Check colour contrast between text and background meets standards.

Logo size and positioning

The logo should be 300px wide on a full width graphic (1024px), appear inside the footer and aligned top right.
