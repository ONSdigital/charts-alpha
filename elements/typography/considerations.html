Readable, accessible and legible letterforms:

  • simple letter forms
  • open counters
  • open terminals
  • extended tails
  • tall ascenders
  • clearly defined letters and numbers (for example, 1lI)
  • balanced spacing
  • large x-height
  • line thickness

Works well:

  • at small sizes
  • on different devices
  • in stressful conditions (for example, bright sunlight, from a distance)

Works with all content types:

  • data viz (for example, calculators, charts and maps)
  • numbers
  • tables
  • long written articles
  • forms
  • multi-language (for example, Welsh)

Performance:

  • system fonts versus web fonts
  • download speed

Consistency:

  • with brand
  • across all products and services

Elements of typography:

  • contrast
  • foreground and background colour
  • vertical rhythm
  • white space
  • weight
  • font size
  • hierarchy
  • measure
