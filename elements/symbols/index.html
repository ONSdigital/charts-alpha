Context

Mathematical symbols and special characters are used throughout the ONS website in articles, data tables and charts.

We are concerned about balancing readability, accessibility (especially screen readers), and understanding.

Common symbols include:

  • Plus: +
  • Minus: −
  • Equals: =
  • Slash: /
  • Ampersand: &
