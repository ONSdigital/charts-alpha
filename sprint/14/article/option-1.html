Have you ever wondered who earns more: a pilot or a doctor?

This interactive allows you to explore the data within the 2014 Annual Survey of Hours and Earnings release. It illustrates the occupations with the highest and lowest earnings, allows you to compare occupations with similar salaries and even tests your knowledge via a couple of quizzes.
