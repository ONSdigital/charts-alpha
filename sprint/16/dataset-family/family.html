The ASHE results tables provide estimates on the levels and distribution of earnings and hours for employees in the UK. Estimates are available for a variety of breakdowns by age groups, industry, occupation, public / private sectors and a range of geographies. All datasets contain estimates for all employees in the UK and each combination of male / female and full-time / part-time employees.
