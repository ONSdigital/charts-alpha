Summary

Police recorded 39,598 offences involving a knife or sharp instrument in the year ending December 2017. About half of these offences were recorded as “assault with injury” and “assault with intent to cause serious harm”. All offence categories for which we collect data showed increases.

This is supported by data for NHS hospitals in England, which showed a 7% increase in admissions for assault by a sharp object in the year ending March 2017.
