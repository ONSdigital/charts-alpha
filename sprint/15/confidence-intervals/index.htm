Learn more about data reliability

  • Estimates vary in quality, mainly reflecting the size of the samples
  • Figures for numbers of jobs are not reliable estimates. These are indicative counts, intended to provide a broad idea of the numbers of employee jobs.
  • For every table showing estimates of earnings or hours, we also publish a corresponding table which provides quality information about those estimates. The quality tables are titled 'Coefficients of variation' (CV).
  • The CV gives an indication of how much an estimate might vary if different samples had been drawn, rather than the particular sample realised on this occasion.
  • The smaller the CV, the greater the accuracy of the estimate.

Read the Quality and Methodology Information for more on data reliability.
