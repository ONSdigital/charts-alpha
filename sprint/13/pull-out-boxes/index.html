User need

  • I need unfamilier concepts explained to me, in a way that doesn't interupt the flow of the content

Prototypes

  • All options on one page
  • Option 1 (grey box)
  • Option 2 (white box with border)
  • Option 3 (inset box)
  • Option 4 (show/hide)
  • Option 5 (footnote)

Research findings

  • The placement and style of the box (option 1) meant that most users noticed it straight away
  • When questioned, users understood that the content was secondary and the styling matched their expectations of the content inside
  • When asked, no users felt that the similartiy to the conact details box box was an issue
  • Most users didn't expect the show/hide box to reveal content, so they were hesitant to click it
